Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I submit a CVET declaration?

Navigate to the CVET Dashboard from the Services menu, fill in the required information across all steps, and submit your declaration for processing.

What is the processing time for applications?

Processing times vary by application type. CVET declarations typically take 2-3 business days, while Direct Assessment may take 3-5 business days.

How can I track my application status?

Visit the Applications section in the sidebar to view your applications by status (Draft, Submitted, Under Review, Approved, Rejected, Completed).

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and credit card payments. Payment options are available when your application is ready for payment.
